• Instructions
  • Create Graph

The "Create Graph" tab opens an empty canvas with the “Add Vertices and Edges” radio button automatically selected at the bottom of the window. 

  1. You can click anywhere on the canvas and drag the cursor to create an edge. 

  2. By clicking and dragging the cursor you can add new vertices and edges. 

  3. You can create an isolated vertex by double-clicking anywhere on the canvas.

  4. To create an edge connecting two existing vertices click on one of the vertices and drag the cursor to the other vertex.

  5. You can connect the same two vertices more than once to create multiple edges. (When you do this, the edges will automatically change and become curved.)

  6. You can create a loop at a vertex by clicking on the vertex and dragging the cursor back to the vertex. (You just have to drag the cursor a little bit away from the vertex and then back.)

  7. By selecting the “Move Vertices” button you can move the vertices around. Click on a vertex and drag it to a new position. The vertex and all edges incident to it will move accordingly.

  8. The “Tallies” window shows a running tally of the total number of vertices, the number of even and odd vertices, the number of edges and the sum of the degrees of the vertices of the graph.

  9. The vertices are labeled A, B, C, etc. in the order they are created. You can change the label of any vertex by double-clicking on the vertex. A popup window will allow you to enter a new label or keep the old label. If you don’t want to see the labels, check the “Hide Labels” box.

  10. If you check the “Show Degrees” box, the degrees will appear by each vertex and the odd vertices will be circled in red.

  11. At any time you can undo your last move by clicking the “Undo Last” button. If you want to start from scratch you can erase all your work by clicking the “Reset” button.
